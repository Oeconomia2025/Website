Oeconomia Governance Protocol

Central Coordination: The governance protocol serves as the foundational layer for the entire network, enabling community-driven decision making through OECgrd Guardian staking and transparent voting mechanisms.

OEC token holders become "Guardians" by staking their tokens to receive OECgrd (OEC Guardian) tokens, which represent both their commitment to network security and their governance participation rights.

Governance Guide

Complete user instructions for Oeconomia Protocol governance through Tally.xyz

Work-Based Governance System where active participation is required to influence protocol decisions across five interconnected protocols through the OECgrd Guardian mechanism.

🏛️ Governance Overview

Oeconomia operates a work-based governance system where active participation is required to influence protocol decisions. The system coordinates governance across five interconnected protocols through the OECgrd Guardian mechanism.

Governed Protocols

Oeconomia Core

Cross-protocol coordination and treasury management

Alluria

Lending protocol (ETH → ALUD stablecoin)

Eloqura

AMM, Bridge, and Yield Optimization

Artivya

Professional trading and NFT marketplace

Iridescia

Smart contract deployment infrastructure

🎯 Getting Started: OEC to OECgrd Conversion

Step 1: Obtain OEC Tokens

Step 1
  • Purchase OEC tokens through supported exchanges
  • Ensure you have ETH for gas fees
  • Connect your wallet to the Oeconomia protocol interface

Step 2: Convert to Guardian Status

Step 2
Required Actions:
1. Navigate to "Guardian Conversion" in the protocol interface
2. Select conversion amount (minimum: 1,000 OEC recommended)
3. Choose your commitment tier:
   - Standard: 1x voting power, basic requirements
   - Enhanced: 1.5x voting power, regular participation needed
   - Elite: 2x voting power, continuous network support required
4. Confirm transaction and pay gas fees
5. Receive OECgrd tokens (1:1 conversion ratio)

Step 3: Activate Governance Rights

Step 3
  • Complete identity verification process
  • Accept Guardian responsibilities agreement
  • Set up notification preferences for proposals
  • Join the community Discord/forum for governance discussions

📝 Proposal Types and Voting Process

Standard Proposals

Duration: 7 days voting period
Quorum: 10% of total OECgrd supply must participate
Threshold: 60% approval required

Examples:

  • Adjusting Alluria collateral ratios
  • Modifying Eloqura AMM curve parameters
  • Updating Artivya trading fees
  • Adding new Iridescia deployment templates

Emergency Proposals

Duration: 24-48 hours
Quorum: 5% of total OECgrd supply
Threshold: 70% approval required

Examples:

  • Pausing protocol functions due to security issues
  • Activating emergency liquidation procedures
  • Implementing temporary parameter changes
  • Coordinating cross-protocol security responses

Treasury Proposals

Duration: 14 days
Quorum: 15% of total OECgrd supply
Threshold: 65% approval required

Examples:

  • Funding development bounties
  • Paying for security audits
  • Supporting ecosystem projects
  • Compensating active contributors

Constitutional Changes

Duration: 21 days
Quorum: 25% of total OECgrd supply
Threshold: 75% approval required

Examples:

  • Modifying governance structure
  • Changing voting thresholds
  • Adding new protocol to the ecosystem
  • Altering fundamental tokenomics

Voting Process

Voting Steps
  1. Review proposal details and community discussion
  2. Check impact assessment and risk analysis
  3. Cast your vote: For, Against, or Abstain
  4. Add optional comment explaining your reasoning
  5. Confirm transaction (small gas fee required)

🎯 Step-by-Step Voting Guide

Before Voting

Preparation
  1. Read the Full Proposal
    • Review technical specifications
    • Understand financial implications
    • Check security audit results (if applicable)
  2. Research Community Sentiment
    • Read forum discussions
    • Join community calls
    • Review expert analysis from other Guardians
  3. Assess Impact
    • Consider effects on all five protocols
    • Evaluate risk/reward ratio
    • Think about long-term consequences

During Voting

Voting Process
  1. Navigate to Tally Proposal Page
  2. Click "Vote" Button
  3. Select Your Choice:
    • For: Support the proposal
    • Against: Oppose the proposal
    • Abstain: Acknowledge without taking a position
  4. Add Comment (Recommended):
    • Explain your reasoning
    • Share relevant expertise
    • Suggest modifications if voting against
  5. Confirm Transaction:
    • Review gas fees
    • Confirm with wallet
    • Wait for transaction confirmation

After Voting

Follow-up
  1. Monitor Results: Track voting progress and community sentiment
  2. Engage in Discussion: Respond to questions about your vote
  3. Prepare for Implementation: If proposal passes, understand your role in execution

🚨 Emergency Governance Procedures

Critical Alert System: Emergency procedures activate during security incidents, protocol failures, or time-sensitive operational issues requiring immediate Guardian response.

When Emergencies Occur

Emergency Response
  1. Alert Notification: Guardians receive immediate notifications
  2. Rapid Assessment: 2-hour period for initial community analysis
  3. Emergency Proposal: Fast-track proposal creation and submission
  4. Expedited Voting: 24-48 hour voting window
  5. Immediate Implementation: Automated execution upon approval

Your Emergency Responsibilities

  • Monitor Alerts: Keep notifications enabled at all times
  • Rapid Response: Vote within 12 hours of emergency proposal
  • Communication: Share critical information with other Guardians
  • Technical Support: Provide expertise during crisis resolution

🔄 Delegation and Proxy Voting

When to Consider Delegation

  • Extended absence from governance activities
  • Lack of technical expertise for specific proposals
  • Time constraints preventing proper research
  • Trust in another Guardian's expertise

Delegation Process

Delegation Steps
  1. Choose Delegate: Select trusted, active Guardian
  2. Set Delegation: Use Tally.xyz delegation interface
  3. Confirm Terms: Agree on delegation scope and duration
  4. Monitor Activity: Track how your votes are being used
  5. Revoke if Needed: Cancel delegation at any time

Finding Quality Delegates

Review Voting History

Check past decisions and reasoning behind votes

Assess Expertise

Look for relevant technical knowledge and experience

Community Standing

Consider reputation and contributions to ecosystem

Alignment

Ensure philosophical agreement on protocol direction

❓ Troubleshooting Common Issues

Voting Problems

Can't see OECgrd balance on Tally

Solution: Refresh page, check wallet connection, verify contract permissions

Vote transaction fails

Solution: Increase gas limit, check ETH balance, retry during lower network congestion

Voting power not displaying correctly

Solution: Wait for tier verification, contact Guardian support, refresh tier status

Account Issues

Lost Guardian status

Solution: Check participation requirements, complete missing activities, contact support

Delegation not working

Solution: Verify delegate address, check delegation contract permissions, re-delegate

Rewards not received

Solution: Verify claim transaction, check participation requirements, submit support ticket

🎯 Best Practices for Effective Governance

Research and Preparation

  • Stay informed about protocol development
  • Understand technical details of each protocol
  • Review past governance decisions and outcomes
  • Network with knowledgeable Guardians

Voting Excellence

  • Never vote without understanding the full proposal
  • Consider effects on each protocol and user group
  • Avoid last-minute decisions, allow time for reflection
  • Explain your reasoning to help others understand

Community Leadership

  • Mentor new Guardians and share knowledge
  • Create quality proposals addressing real problems
  • Facilitate discussion to reach informed consensus
  • Bridge protocols and share insights across communities

Long-term Thinking

  • Consider protocol sustainability over short-term gains
  • Support decisions that strengthen governance participation
  • Prioritize security and innovation
  • Think beyond individual protocols to entire ecosystem

Success Formula: Consistent participation + informed decision-making + community collaboration = effective governance and maximum rewards

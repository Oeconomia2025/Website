How to Buy OEC Token

1
 Set Up Your Wallet

Download and install a compatible crypto wallet that supports ERC-20 tokens. We recommend MetaMask for beginners or Trust Wallet for mobile users.

MetaMask Trust Wallet

2
 Get Ethereum (ETH)

Purchase Ethereum from a reputable exchange like Coinbase, Binance, or Kraken. You'll need ETH to pay for gas fees and to swap for OEC tokens.

Coinbase Binance Kraken

3
 Connect to DEX

Connect your wallet to a decentralized exchange (DEX) such as Uniswap or SushiSwap. Make sure you're on the official website to avoid scams.

Uniswap SushiSwap

4
 Add Token Contract

Add OEC token to your swap interface using our contract address. Always verify the contract address matches exactly to avoid fake tokens.

5
 Swap ETH for OEC

Enter the amount of ETH you want to swap for OEC tokens. Review the transaction details, including gas fees, before confirming the swap.

6
 Confirm & Hold

Confirm the transaction in your wallet and wait for it to be processed on the blockchain. Your OEC tokens will appear in your wallet once confirmed.

⚠️ Important Safety Tips

• Always double-check contract addresses to avoid fake tokens
• Never share your private keys or seed phrase with anyone
• Start with small amounts to test the process
• Be aware of gas fees, especially during network congestion
• Only use official websites and verified links
